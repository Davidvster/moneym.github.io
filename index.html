About the app

MoneyM is a personal money tracking app for quickly logging expenses and income, seeing where your money goes, and spotting patterns in your spending over time.

Your data is stored locally on your device and works fully offline — no account required. Turn on the optional Google Drive backup to keep it safe and in sync across your devices, and lock the app behind a PIN or biometrics. There are no ads and no third-party tracking SDKs.

What is MoneyM? ▾
MoneyM is a personal money tracking app for quickly logging expenses and income, seeing where your money goes, and spotting patterns in your spending over time.
Where is my data stored? ▾
By default, all your data lives locally on your device and the app works offline. If you enable the optional backup, backup files are kept in a private app folder in your own Google Drive so you can restore or sync across devices — and you can protect them with a password so they are encrypted. There is no central MoneyM server holding your data.
How does bank sync work? ▾
Bank sync is optional and uses PSD2 open banking through Enable Banking with your own free key. Imported transactions travel directly between your device and your bank and appear as suggestions you review before they are added — nothing is routed through a MoneyM server.
Is MoneyM free? ▾
Yes — MoneyM is free to use, with no ads and no third-party tracking SDKs.
